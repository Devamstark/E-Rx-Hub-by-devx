# Pharmacy User Manual: DevXWorld e-Rx Hub

**Welcome, Pharmacist.**
The e-Rx Hub transforms your pharmacy into a digitally connected healthcare node. This guide covers how to receive digital prescriptions, manage your inventory, and maintain compliant records.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Registration & Verification
1.  **Sign Up:** Select "Pharmacy" on the login screen and click "New Registration".
2.  **Details:** Enter your Establishment Name, License Number (Form 20/21), and Address.
3.  **Documents:** You **must** upload a valid Pharmacy License.
4.  **Verification:** Your account will be reviewed by Admins. Access is granted only after verification.

### Login
*   Use your registered email and password.
*   **OTP:** Enter the 6-digit OTP sent to your registered mobile.

---

## 2. Dashboard Overview
Your dashboard is the command center for daily operations:
*   **Top Bar:** Displays total Inventory Value and Low Stock Alerts.
*   **E-Rx Management:** The live queue of incoming prescriptions.
*   **Inventory:** Manage stock, batches, and pricing.
*   **Reports:** Financial overview and dispensing logs.

---

## 3. Dispensing Prescriptions (Workflow)

### Step 1: Check the Queue
*   Navigate to **E-Rx Management** -> **Pending Queue**.
*   You will see prescriptions sent specifically to your pharmacy by Doctors.
*   Click **Process** on a prescription to begin.

### Step 2: Patient Matching (Critical)
*   The system will attempt to match the incoming prescription to your existing customer database.
*   **Match Found:** Select the existing patient profile to link the record.
*   **No Match:** Select "Create New Profile". Verify the patient's Phone Number and Address to create a permanent record.

### Step 3: Dispense or Reject
*   **Dispense:** Once the patient is linked and medicines are verified, click **Confirm & Dispense**. This updates the status to "DISPENSED" for the doctor.
*   **Out of Stock:** If you cannot fulfill the order, click the **No Stock** button. The doctor will be instantly notified to issue an alternative.
*   **Reject:** Use for other issues (e.g., duplicate orders).

---

## 4. Inventory Management

### Adding Stock
1.  Go to the **Inventory** tab.
2.  Click **Add Item**.
3.  **Details:** Enter Medicine Name, Batch No, Expiry, MRP, and Quantity.
4.  **Narcotics:** If the drug is a controlled substance (NDPS), check the "Narcotic / Controlled?" box. This flags the item for audit.

### Managing Stock
*   **Low Stock:** Items below the minimum level trigger an alert on your main dashboard.
*   **Delete:** Remove expired or obsolete items using the trash icon.

---

## 5. Reports & Analytics
*   Navigate to **Reports**.
*   **Financials:** View estimated revenue and profit based on dispensed prescriptions.
*   **Activity Log:** A chronological list of all actions taken (Dispensed, Rejected) for audit purposes.

---

## 6. Doctor Directory
*   Maintain a contact list of local doctors for quick queries regarding prescriptions.
*   Go to **Doctors** -> **Add Contact** to save details (Phone, Hospital, Specialty).
